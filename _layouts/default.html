Research

My research interest is 3D/4D Computer Vision and its application in Robotics. Specifically, I would love to explore building 3D perception models from vast amounts of unlabeled data, with a structural understanding of the world to support spatial and temporal structural reasoning abilities. Ultimately, my goal is to create intelligent agents with human-like perception abilities. Selected works are listed below (*equal contribution).
